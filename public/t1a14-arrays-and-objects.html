Objects: a named list of variables, such as properties of a certain thing (example: An object, a person, and their different defining characteristics.

Arrays: a numbered list of variables, such as many of a certain kind of thing (example: A box of fruits, and the different kinds of fruits inside of it.


An Array can exist inside of an object (example: A person's different characteristics, their favorite kinds of fruits) An object can exist inside of an Array (example: A box of fruits, and the texture or age of a particular fruit)
